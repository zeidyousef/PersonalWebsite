Projects

Water Level Alarm Circuit Using AutoCAD


This circuit is useful for many possible uses including monitoring a sump pit (to control pump activation), rainfall detection, and leakage detection. Electronic water level circuits have the capability of alerting if there is a water leak. When the water level is too high or exceeds the higher limit, it can detect the water level easily by hearing an alarm sound. We can also measure the fuel level in motor vehicles and the liquid level containers.

Li-ion Battery Charger PCB


This circuit is responsible for charging a single cell Li-ion safely using a charging IC and allows the circuit to be used while charging, or unplugged. Even when using the circuit while charging, the battery will still charge at full speed, assuming the input power supply can supply enough power. Also, Li-ion battery charger ICs are useful because they can regulate battery charging current and voltage.

H-Bridge Motor Controller Arduino PCB


This circuit covers the selection and schematic design of a ~800mA H- Bridge to be used with a separate MCU such as a Raspberry Pi, or an Arduino. This circuit considers the Back EMF dangers of an inductive load and handles them in two separate ways. Also, it covers the PCB layout itself and how I designed the board and placed all of the components. The TC78H620FNG Dual- bridge driver IC was used which incorporates DMOS in output transistors. It’s capable of driving 2 DC brushed motors or 1 stepping motor which helped us in performing the electric SAE formula vehicle. Some of the features that the Toshiba IC has are it is built-in over current detection (ISD), thermal shutdown (TSD) circuit, and under voltage lockout (UVLO) circuit.

FSAE BATTERY MANAGEMENT SYSTEM(BMS)


Orion BMS 2 was used as a battery management system for the battery packs since it’s useful for protecting and managing battery packs for electric vehicles (EV) plug-in hybrid (PHEV) and hybrid vehicles (HEV) with automotive grade quality. My main goal was to configure the Battery Management system to communicate appropriately with other ECUs in the vehicle by figuring out the whole wiring process and how it works.
